## Key Insights

1. **19.5M registered, ~10K MAU** — 0.05% monthly active rate. The vast majority came from a single referral-landing campaign and never returned.

2. **175K ever submitted work** (0.9% of registered) — this is the real "worker" base. Of those, only 390 were active in the last 90 days.

3. **Binance ecosystem dominance** — 84% BNB Chain wallets, 127K quiz participants, suggesting a Binance-driven growth campaign.

4. **89.9% adoption rate** on submissions — quality is high among active workers (low dispute rate).

5. **Social binding = engagement proxy** — 32.6K users bound social accounts (X, Discord, Telegram). This cohort likely overlaps heavily with active submitters.

6. **Zero KYC, 1 staker, 835 DIDs** — advanced identity features have near-zero adoption.

7. **Realistic addressable workforce for task launching**: ~23K users active in past 180d, ~400 in 90d. New task campaigns should plan for this scale, not the 19.5M headline number.
